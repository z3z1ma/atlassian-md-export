# Live Confluence Page 4185325571 Export Verification

## What Was Observed

Live Confluence export of page `4185325571` initially failed because `/wiki/api/v2/pages/4185325571/ancestors` returned ancestor summary objects with only `id` and `type`; one ancestor was a folder. Live CLI verification then exposed two additional correctness defects in the same path:

- The v2 page payload had `spaceId` but no raw `spaceKey`, causing exact page exports to write under `pages/unknown-space/`.
- The page payload had `_links.base: https://floqast.atlassian.net/wiki` and `_links.webui: /spaces/DP/pages/...`; normal URL joining dropped `/wiki`.
